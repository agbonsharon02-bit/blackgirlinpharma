Our Mission

The Black Girl In Pharma Movement

Black Girl In Pharma is a movement dedicated to increasing visibility, representation, and opportunity for Black women in the pharmaceutical industry.

From regulatory affairs and clinical research to pharmacovigilance and biotechnology, we spotlight the incredible range of careers available in pharma and provide the mentorship, community, and resources needed to thrive.

We believe that when Black women succeed in pharma, the entire industry benefits. Diverse perspectives drive better science, better medicine, and better outcomes for patients worldwide.

Pharma Career Spotlight

The pharmaceutical industry offers extraordinary careers. Here are some of the most impactful paths you can pursue.

Regulatory Affairs

Guide drug and medical device approvals through FDA and global regulatory agencies. Ensure compliance with evolving regulations.

Avg. Salary $85K – $105K CAD
Key Skills Regulatory strategy, documentation, FDA guidelines
Full Career Guide

Clinical Research

Design and manage clinical trials that bring life-saving treatments to patients. A cornerstone of pharmaceutical innovation.

Avg. Salary $88K – $108K CAD
Key Skills Trial management, GCP, data analysis
Full Career Guide

Quality Assurance

Ensure every product meets the highest safety and quality standards. Protect patients through rigorous testing and audits.

Avg. Salary $55K – $105K CAD
Key Skills GMP, auditing, CAPA, quality systems
Full Career Guide

Medical Affairs

Bridge the gap between science and commercial strategy. Serve as the medical voice within pharmaceutical companies.

Avg. Salary $112K – $135K CAD
Key Skills Medical science, KOL management, communication
Full Career Guide

Pharmacovigilance

Monitor drug safety after market approval. Detect, assess, and prevent adverse effects to protect public health.

Avg. Salary $80K – $148K CAD
Key Skills Signal detection, safety reporting, MedDRA
Full Career Guide

Pharma Sales

Connect healthcare providers with breakthrough treatments. Build relationships and drive access to innovative medicines.

Avg. Salary $88K – $120K CAD
Key Skills Sales strategy, relationship building, market knowledge
Full Career Guide

Market Access

Develop strategies to ensure patients can access and afford new therapies. Navigate pricing, reimbursement, and policy.

Avg. Salary $108K – $128K CAD
Key Skills Health economics, pricing strategy, payer relations
Full Career Guide

Biotechnology

Pioneer next-generation therapies using cutting-edge science. From gene therapy to biologics, shape the future of medicine.

Avg. Salary $65K – $95K CAD
Key Skills Molecular biology, bioprocessing, R&D
Full Career Guide

Manufacturing

Oversee the production of pharmaceutical products at scale. Ensure efficiency, safety, and compliance in operations.

Avg. Salary $62K – $90K CAD
Key Skills Process engineering, GMP, supply chain
Full Career Guide
